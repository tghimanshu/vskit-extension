Privacy Policy for VSKit Auto Unmute and Next

Last updated: April 18, 2026

VSKit Auto Unmute and Next (the "Extension") is designed to improve playback convenience on VSKit watch pages by auto-unmuting videos and moving to the next episode when playback ends.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

  • No account data collection
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies created by the Extension
  • No remote servers or third-party API calls

How the Extension Works

The Extension runs only on these pages:

  • https://vskit.tv/watch/*
  • https://www.vskit.tv/watch/*

On those pages, it reads page elements (such as video and episode controls) and URL parameters (for example, ep) locally in your browser to apply playback behavior. This processing stays on-device and is not transmitted elsewhere.

Data Storage

The Extension does not use browser storage for user data and does not persist personal information.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not send data to third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect extension changes or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions about this Extension, use the developer support contact listed in the browser extension store listing.
